Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 02, 2026

Welcome to UK Exam Pro. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

UK Exam Pro is designed to be a privacy-friendly application. We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information like name, email, or phone number to use the basic features of the app.

  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information about how the app is used, such as quiz scores (stored locally) and sync timestamps.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information like device model and OS version may be processed by third-party services (like AdMob) to ensure app compatibility.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can manage ad settings in your Google Account.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app updates and integrity checks.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • Internet Access: To sync the question library from our servers.
  • Network State: To check if an internet connection is available before syncing.
  • Storage (Optional): To allow users to export or import quiz data if requested.

4. Data Security

All your quiz progress and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal quiz data on our servers. When you sync the library, we only download question data; no personal data is uploaded.

5. Children's Privacy

UK Exam Pro does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you think your child provided this kind of information on our app, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app's version.
